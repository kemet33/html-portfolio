THE TOP 10 TV SHOWS ACCORDING TO LOVET FON

My top 10 TV shows of all time

1. Star Trek: The Next Generation



The reason why this is one of my favorite is the different story line each episode brings while keeping to a general story line. This show will broaden your view of what may be out there if mankind was to take to the sky to explore the known galaxy while meeting different species and forming alliances. This show is for the science inclined imaginative person.

2. Babylon 5



Babylon 5 is an epic show of action, betrayal, politics and ancient races with technologies that makes them seems like gods to the younger races. The older races both fight using the younger races as proxy for dominance on who thinks it is the best way to teach and noture the younger races. The Vorlons thinks love and spirituality is best to bring up the younger races while the Shadows uses wars and brutality.

3. Doctor Who



Doctor who is another epic science fiction of a time lord capable of traveling backward and forward in time both into the future and the past fighting and preventing powerful enemies from destroying other worlds especially his favorite planet, Earth. His time-traveling machine is called the TARDIS which stands for Time And Relative Dimension In Space. The TARDIS looks like a police phone box which is smaller on the outside but enormously bigger in the inside. The time lord is called the Doctor, he is able to regenerate and he is an ancient being possesing two hearts.
